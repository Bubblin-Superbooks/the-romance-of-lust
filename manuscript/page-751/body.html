Turning the talk on her suspicions of having got with child at the morning prayers, I remarked that she had had only one child by her husband, and as he had lived many years after Harry’s birth, and from what she said, she had continued to be enjoyed by him, it was, therefore, not probable she would now be got in the family-way.

“That appears probable, my dear boy, but then he took precaution not to get any more children.”

“But what precautions could he take, and how did he do so?”
